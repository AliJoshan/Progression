About Progression

A minimal tool for tracking what matters. No noise. No gamification. Just clarity on whether you're moving forward.

What Progression Is

Progression is a personal dashboard for tracking daily tasks, goals, consistency, and long-term progress. It provides a clear view of your efforts over time — emphasizing discipline and visibility rather than motivation or instant gratification.

The Problem It Solves

Many people stay busy without knowing if they are truly progressing. Days blur together, efforts feel scattered, and there's no honest record of what was actually accomplished. Progression exists to make progress measurable, honest, and visible over time.
